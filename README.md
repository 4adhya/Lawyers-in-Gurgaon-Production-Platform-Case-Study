# Lawyers in Gurgaon – Legal Facilitation Website

A professional legal facilitation platform built to help individuals connect with independent practicing advocates in Gurugram (Gurgaon) for legal consultation, documentation guidance, and matter-specific support.

## 🌐 Live Website

Main Website: https://www.lawyersingurgaon.in
Legal Team Page: https://www.lawyersingurgaon.in/legal-team-gurgaon/

## 📌 About the Project

This website is designed as a legal facilitation platform focused on making legal access simple, structured, and practical for users in Gurgaon and nearby areas.

It helps visitors:

* Understand legal processes before taking action
* Reach the relevant practice area quickly
* Connect through WhatsApp-based consultation flow
* Access service-specific legal guidance pages
* View advocate profiles and legal team information

Important: This platform does **not operate as a law firm** and does **not directly provide legal representation**. Representation, drafting, and court-related work are handled independently after professional assessment.
